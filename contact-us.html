Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Change or Cancel my booking?

  • Please contact us on 0871 345 2000* – say the word ‘Operator’ and you will be connected to a member of our team to discuss your booking. Please ensure you have your booking reference available where possible.


    • I’ve Lost something at the Cinema

  • We’ll do our very best to reunite you with it. Please call 0871 200 2000* – say the word ‘Operator’ and you will be connected to a member of our team who will assist you.


    • Where is the Pin Number on the Gift-Card?

  • Next to the long 19 digit card number on the back you’ll see a scratch panel – simply scratch this off and you’ll see the 4 digit number.


    • I’ve received a CWB Error Code when trying to make a booking.

  • Ooops, we’re sorry to hear that. It’s more than likely a time out, payment or connection error - please try and make your booking again.
